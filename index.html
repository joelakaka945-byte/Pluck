How to use Pluck - Tap to Open
Answer: Paste a public link and Pluck asks your private engine for a direct file. You can preview then save.
Public posts from Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X and Reddit. Private or login-only content cannot be accessed.
  • 1. Copy link via Share
  • 2. Paste and tap Download
  • 3. Preview, then choose save method for your device
Choose Auto for video with audio, or Audio only to get MP3. Filenames are created locally.
Your link is sent only to your engine. No storage, no watermark, files are streamed directly to you.
Reloads a fresh copy with a timestamp to clear cache.

Error Guide Help & Fixes

Quick reference for common messages.

Link Not Supported

Use a public link from the supported platforms.

Private or Unavailable

If you cannot view it in incognito, the engine cannot either.

Age Restricted

Requires login, not supported.

Bad URL

Link must start with https://

Engine Warming Up

Tap Wake Engine and wait a few seconds.

No File Returned

Livestream, DRM or expired story. Try a different quality.

Timeout

Network issue. Refresh and try again.
