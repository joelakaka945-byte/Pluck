How This Engine Works

This frontend connects directly to your private Cobalt instance hosted on Railway. It does not store or process your links on any third-party server.

Common Issues & Fixes

  • “Warming up” or “CORS blocked”: Your Railway app sleeps after inactivity. Press “Wake Engine” and wait 5–8 seconds. Make sure you have set:
CORS = *
API_URL = https://cobalt-production-2c27.up.railway.app
Go to Railway → Variables → Add them → Click Deploy.
  • “Cannot POST /api/json”: This endpoint is deprecated. This app now uses only POST /, which is correct.
  • Download doesn’t start on iPhone?: Use “Blob Save”. Then go to Files App → Downloads to find your file.
  • Link looks cut off?: On iOS, copy from the Share menu, not the address bar.

Privacy Note

No data is saved. Your link is sent directly to your Cobalt engine. This page works offline once loaded.
